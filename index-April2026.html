Sankalp (संकल्प)

A profound Sanskrit word meaning a solemn vow, a resolve, a purposeful intention — the deepest form of commitment to a cause. It captures the spirit of using science to protect lives and serve humanity.

About

From Equations
to Impact

Dr. Saptadeep Biswas is a dedicated researcher and academician with a Ph.D. in Mathematics from NIT Agartala (2021–2025), awarded under the prestigious DST INSPIRE Fellowship.

His doctoral thesis — A Study on Intelligent Disaster Management: Implementing AI-based Prediction and Logistics Optimization Techniques — bridges artificial intelligence and real-world humanitarian operations.

Currently serving as Assistant Professor at GITAM School of Business, he leads the SANKALP Lab, bringing deep expertise in evolutionary computation, multi-objective optimization, and supply chain resilience.

Research Focus

Three Pillars of
SANKALP

Publishing consistently in Q1 SCIE journals with impact factors up to 13.9, the SANKALP Lab operates at the intersection of intelligent algorithms, disaster management, and optimization science — a vow made real through peer-reviewed scholarship.

01 ——

Intelligent Disaster Management

AI-driven frameworks for earthquake relief demand forecasting, flood prediction, seismic vulnerability mapping, and community resilience. Case studies in Türkiye and India. IJDRR (Q1, IF 4.5).

→
02 ——

Metaheuristic Optimization

Designing novel hybrid evolutionary algorithms integrating Differential Evolution with Gazelle, Coati, Crayfish, and Grey Wolf optimizers for global and constrained engineering problems. CMAME (Q1, IF 7.3), AI Review (Q1, IF 13.9).

→
03 ——

Sustainable Supply Chains

Multi-objective optimization of agro-food networks, humanitarian logistics using spherical fuzzy methodology, and resource allocation in quarantine centers. JCP (Q1, IF 10.0), ANOR (Q1, IF 4.5).
